## Introduction

AuraWell ("we", "our", or "us") operates the AuraWell mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

## Information Collection and Use

### Personal Information

We may collect the following types of information:

- **Account Information**: Name, email address
- **Health Data**: Mood entries, journal entries, meditation sessions (stored locally on your device)
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the App
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version

### Local Storage

All personal health data (mood logs, journal entries, meditation sessions) is stored **locally on your device** using browser localStorage. We do not transmit this data to our servers.

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not sell your personal information
- We do not share your health data with third parties
- We do not track your location
- We do not access your contacts or photos without permission

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

- Providing and maintaining the App
- Improving user experience
- Communicating with you about updates
- Analyzing app usage to improve features

## Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

- Health data is stored locally on your device only
- We use industry-standard encryption for any data transmitted
- Account credentials are securely hashed

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
